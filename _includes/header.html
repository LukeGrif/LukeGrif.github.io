About

I am a recent First-Class Honours graduate in Electronic & Computer Engineering from the University of Limerick, now embarking on an exciting PhD journey with the Centre for Robotics & Intelligent Systems (CRIS) at UL. My research will focus on Autonomous Surface Vehicles (ASV) and Integrated Approaches for Reef Habitat Assessment and Environmental DNA Sampling.

With hands-on experience from internships at Analog Devices, Fiserv, and ACI Worldwide—paired with a strong academic record in Electronic & Computer Engineering—I bring a well-rounded skill set spanning hardware, software, and data-driven solutions.
